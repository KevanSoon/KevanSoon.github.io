HelpLah.AI

HelpLah.AI is a multilingual AI chatbot developed during Hack n Roll 2025 to address social isolation and loneliness among Singaporeans, particularly youth. Built with Flask and powered by the robust Llama-2-7B-chat-GGML model, it communicates effectively in both English and Mandarin. The chatbot uses the gtts library for natural-sounding voice responses, fostering empathetic and engaging interactions. To ensure efficient performance, the system connects the Flask application to Google Colabâ€™s corporate GPUs via ngrok, enabling fast response times without relying on local hardware. HelpLah.AI provides accessible mental health support, showcasing the potential of technology in promoting well-being and community connection.

GreenMerlion.AI

This project addresses low recycling rates by combining image processing, AI, and gamification to promote better recycling practices. The Waste Classification System analyzes photos of waste to classify items as recyclable or non-recyclable, reducing contamination in recycling bins and improving recycling efficiency. An AI-Powered Assistant provides users with guidance on proper waste disposal and answers questions about recyclable materials, making it easier to recycle correctly. To encourage active participation, a Gamification System rewards users for correct recycling practices, fostering long-term behavioral change and building a community focused on responsible waste management.

Carapace AI

In this project, my team and I labeled and trained datasets sourced from Kaggle to build a model using Yolov5 for detecting whether healthcare workers are wearing their personal protective equipment (PPE) properly. The system enables real-time monitoring to ensure compliance with safety protocols, enhancing workplace safety. This project showcases my ability to work with large datasets, apply machine learning techniques, and contribute to public health initiatives through AI-driven solutions.

Room Furniture Budget Automation

This project, developed for the AISG Student Hackathon 2021, leverages Python libraries PeekingDuck and TagUI RPA to automate the process of calculating budgets for room furniture based on specified themes. The system efficiently streamlines the budgeting process by extracting furniture options, calculating costs, and organizing the information to match desired room aesthetics. This solution highlights my expertise in integrating automation tools and developing streamlined workflows for complex tasks.
