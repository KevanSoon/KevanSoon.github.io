About Justin Bieber

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter who became a global pop sensation at just 15 years old. Discovered on YouTube by talent manager Scooter Braun, Justin quickly rose to international fame with his debut single "One Time" in 2009.

Born in London, Ontario, and raised in Stratford, Justin taught himself to play piano, drums, guitar, and trumpet. His incredible vocal range and natural musical talent, combined with his boy-next-door charm, captured the hearts of millions of fans worldwide.

From his early days as a teen heartthrob to his evolution as a mature artist, Justin has sold over 150 million records worldwide, making him one of the best-selling music artists of all time.

Musical Journey

Justin's discography spans over a decade of chart-topping hits that have defined pop music for an entire generation. From his debut album "My World" to recent releases, his music has evolved from teen pop to mature R&B and electronic influences.

His breakthrough single "Baby" became one of the most-watched videos on YouTube, while songs like "Sorry," "Love Yourself," and "Peaches" showcase his artistic growth and versatility as a performer.

With multiple Grammy Awards, Billboard Music Awards, and countless other accolades, Justin continues to push musical boundaries and inspire artists worldwide.

Career Highlights

Legacy & Impact

Justin Bieber's career has been marked by incredible milestones and personal growth. From his humble beginnings posting covers on YouTube to becoming one of the most influential artists of the 21st century, his journey has inspired millions.

Beyond music, Justin has used his platform for philanthropy, mental health awareness, and social causes. His openness about personal struggles has helped destigmatize mental health discussions among young people.

Today, Justin continues to evolve as an artist, husband, and role model, proving that with talent, dedication, and authenticity, dreams can become reality.

2008 - YouTube Discovery

12-year-old Justin discovered by talent manager Scooter Braun through YouTube covers, leading to his first record deal with Island Records

2009 - "One Time" Debut

Released debut single "One Time" which peaked at #17 on Billboard Hot 100, launching his career as a teen sensation

2010 - "Baby" Phenomenon

Released "Baby" featuring Ludacris, becoming one of the most-watched YouTube videos and cementing his global stardom

2012 - "Believe" Era

Third studio album "Believe" debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, showcasing his evolution from teen pop to more mature R&B sound

2015 - "Purpose" Comeback

Released critically acclaimed album "Purpose" with hits "What Do You Mean?", "Sorry", and "Love Yourself", marking his artistic renaissance

2018 - Marriage to Hailey

Married model Hailey Baldwin (now Hailey Bieber), marking a new chapter of personal stability and growth

2020 - "Changes" Personal Album

Released deeply personal album "Changes" reflecting on marriage, mental health, and personal growth, featuring hit single "Yummy"

2021 - "Justice" Success

Sixth studio album "Justice" topped charts worldwide with massive hits "Peaches" and "Stay", proving his continued relevance

2023 - Mental Health Advocacy

Became vocal advocate for mental health awareness, sharing his struggles and inspiring millions of fans worldwide
